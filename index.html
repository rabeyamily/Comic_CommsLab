Don Midori vs Don Taichi

On a rain-soaked campus, rival cat gangs led by Don Midori and Don Taichi face off in a tense turf war. As paws twitch and claws flex, Midori asserts his authority and loyalty is tested when their food source is sabotaged. A fierce showdown erupts between the two gangs, only for the epic scene to collapse when a human student casually picks up one of the cats, revealing the absurd reality behind the mob-like standoff.
